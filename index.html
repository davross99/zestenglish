AT / IN / ON

Three tiny words. Almost no rules. Endless mistakes — for all ages, from every country.

AT, IN and ON don't follow a clean rulebook. Learners memorise lists, forget them by the next lesson, and keep saying "in Monday" or "at the kitchen." Prepositions are best learned through practice and repetition.

With Jack and the Temple of Shadows, it's a fun way to challenge yourself and help Jack complete his journey while you master the use of prepositions along the way.

How to play

Tap the correct preposition — AT, IN, or ON — to fill in the gap and proceed.
