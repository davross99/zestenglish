Jack and the Temple of Shadows

Our latest release, Jack and the Temple of Shadows, is a narrative adventure designed to help students master the prepositions of time and place: at, in, and on. Players progress through the story only by using the correct grammar, providing a seamless, drill-free learning experience. Available now on Google Play.

Why This Learning Method Works

Contextual and narrative-based learning significantly improves language acquisition. By placing grammar rules within a story, learners connect words to situations rather than memorizing abstract lists. This method is highly effective for language academies and corporate training environments, such as BPOs and call centers, where precise and accurate English communication is essential. It enhances information retention, increases engagement, and removes the anxiety often associated with traditional grammar drills.

Why a Temple, Why Prepositions

The hardest words in English aren’t verbs. They’re the small ones that describe where and when things are.

These core prepositions define the boundaries of time and place. Learners of all ages can stumble on these because simple rules often fail to cover their usage. The only way they stick is by using them in context: at a specific point, in a defined space or period, and on a surface or scheduled time. The temple forces this precision—if your grammar is wrong, the path is blocked.

  • AT | Specific points: Pinpointing the exact moment or the precise location where Jack must stand to survive.
  • IN | Enclosed spaces and periods: Navigating chambers or enduring timeframes that hold the secrets of the temple.
  • ON | Surfaces and schedules: Stepping onto critical platforms or hitting the timing required to bypass traps.
