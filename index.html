The Challenge

100 gaps. One story. Every answer unambiguous.

Jack's journey is built around 100 fill-in-the-gap moments, each with exactly one correct preposition — no trick questions, no guessing. You learn by doing, scene after scene, until the right word stops being a decision and starts being a reflex.

AT / IN / ON

Three tiny words.
Almost no rules.
Constant mistakes made by learners of every age and from every country.

AT, IN and ON don't follow a clean rulebook. Learners memorise lists, forget them by the next lesson, and keep saying "in Monday" or "at the kitchen." Prepositions are best learned through practice and repetition.

Jack and the Temple of Shadows is a fun way to challenge yourself while helping Jack complete his journey and you master the use of prepositions along the way.

How to play

Simply tap the correct preposition — AT, IN, or ON — to fill in the gap and proceed.
